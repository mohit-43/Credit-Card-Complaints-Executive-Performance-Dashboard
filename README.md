# Credit Card Complaints: Executive Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer friction points and operational efficiency using a credit card complaints dataset sourced from Kaggle. Utilizing Tableau, I developed an executive-level performance dashboard designed to turn raw complaint data into actionable business intelligence. 

The dashboard provides leadership with a high-level view of operational response metrics, geographic distribution of issues, and a granular breakdown of the primary drivers behind customer dissatisfaction.

## Key Metrics & Insights Tracked
*   **Total Volume:** Analyzed **86,893 total complaints** to understand historical trends and workload distribution.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Monitored an outstanding **98.9% Timely Response Rate** against an **Average Resolution Time of 2.869 days**.
*   **Customer Friction:** Tracked a **20.6% Consumer Dispute Rate**, mapping out where customers are most likely to contest resolutions.
*   **Root Cause Analysis:** Identified **Billing Disputes** as the leading category for customer complaints, accounting for **14,688 cases**.

## Features & Functionality
*   **Executive KPI Cards:** At-a-glance performance tracking for volume, responsiveness, dispute ratios, and resolution speeds.
*   **Trend Timeline:** A historical line chart tracking the influx of complaints from 2016 through mid-2021 to pinpoint seasonal surges or systemic shifts.
*   **Geographic Map:** An interactive US map visualizing regional complaint concentrations and dispute rates by state.
*   **Top Issues Bar Chart:** A clean, ordered distribution breakdown highlighting the highest-frequency complaint categories.
*   **Interactive Filters:** Capabilities to slice and dice data dynamically by **State** and **Issue** category.

## Tools & Technologies Used
*   **Data Source:** Kaggle (Credit Card Complaints Dataset)
*   **BI Tool:** Tableau Desktop / Tableau Public
*   **Skills Demonstrated:** Dashboard Design, KPI Formulation, Data Visualization, Root Cause Analysis, Geospatial Mapping
